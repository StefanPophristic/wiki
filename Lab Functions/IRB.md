The current lab manager is in charge of IRB approvals. In case there is no lab manager, you will have to submit materials for approval yourself. 

Between Liina and Alec, we have 4 IRB protocols (as of Spring 2023): 
1. "Online language judgment": All online experiments 
2. "Language processing study": In-person behavioral experiments 
3. "MEG investigation of language comprehension and production": Liina's MEG/fMRI protocol 
4. "MEG study of psychophysics and language processing": Alec's MEG/fMRI protocol 

Each of these protocols has a unique NYU-internal ID. You can ask the lab manager or Alec/Liina for these IDs if you need them. 

Almost all MEG/Online experiments are already covered by these four protocols. Once you are approved for a protocol, you can run experiments. The consent forms for these protocols are relatively general and can be used for most experiments in the lab. You can access your IRB protocols through the NYU Cayuse portal: [https://nyu.cayuse.com/rs/irb/](https://nyu.cayuse.com/rs/irb/#dashboard). To access the relevant protocols, you have to have completed your [CITI training](https://www.nyu.edu/research/resources-and-support-offices/getting-started-withyourresearch/human-subjects-research/tutorial.html).
